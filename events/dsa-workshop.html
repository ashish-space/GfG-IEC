About the Workshop

Are you ready to level up your coding game? IEC College of Engineering and Technology, in collaboration with GeeksforGeeks, presents an exclusive, hands-on offline workshop on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA).

Whether you are preparing for upcoming internships or aiming for top-tier product companies, this offline session is designed to sharpen your problem-solving skills and provide you with a clear roadmap to mastery.

The live seminar will take place in the Seminar Hall, F-Block at IEC College, Greater Noida on 27 February 2026 from 2:00 PM IST.

Topics Covered

  • Time & Space Complexity — Big O, Big Theta, Big Omega
  • Arrays — Operations, Sorting, Searching, Common Patterns
  • Strings — Manipulation, Pattern Matching Basics
  • Linked Lists — Singly, Doubly, Circular; Insertion, Deletion
  • Stacks & Queues — Array and Linked List implementations
  • Recursion & Backtracking — Base cases, call stack, classic problems
  • Binary Trees — Inorder, Preorder, Postorder traversals, Height, Diameter
  • Hashing — HashMap, HashSet, Frequency counting
  • Two Pointers & Sliding Window Technique
  • Greedy Algorithms — Introduction and interview questions
  • Dynamic Programming — Memoization vs Tabulation
  • Placement Roadmap & Competitive Programming Tips
